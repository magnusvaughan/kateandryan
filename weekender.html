Joining us for the weekend?...

If we have invited you to join us for the weekend, we have already booked pitches for you. You are welcome to arrive on the afternoon of Friday 1st August, 2014. We have the space booked until the morning of Monday 4th August. We do have limited space, so please can we ask people not to bring tents that are much bigger than they need.

Food

We have arranged for the campsite to put on a BBQ lunch for us, but please bring your own food for meals other than dinner on Saturday and lunch on Sunday. You can also purchase food onsite should you prefer. The campsite has a shop that sells food and drink and they also sell cooked meals throughout the day.

Music

Our fabulous DJ mates will be playing music all day on Sunday in the tipi. Please bring your guitar for acoustic sessions round the campfire too!
