By car

From Ipswich take A12 North towards Lowestoft. Take the 2nd exit to Wickham Market into the village. Third left onto Spring lane. Please travel at no more than 20 MPH whilst on the lane. The Orchard is about 500 metres up the lane on the left hand side.

From Lowestoft take A12 South towards Ipswich. Take the 1st exit to Wickham Market and proceed as above.

From the West leave the A14 and turn onto the A140 towards Norwich. Pick up the B1078 to Wickham Market. Follow through Coddenham, Ashbocking, Otley, Debach etc until you reach Wickham Market. Turn left and first right into Spring Lane.
